## üìä Strategy & Investment

### How does the "Sentiment Strategy" work?

We use the **Fear & Greed Index** to determine the optimal allocation between Crypto (BTC/ETH) and
Stablecoins.

- When the market is **fearful** (prices low), we allocate more to **Crypto**.
- When the market is **greedy** (prices high), we allocate more to **Stablecoins**.

### How often does it rebalance?

Rebalancing only happens when the market shifts **regimes** (e.g., from Fear to Neutral). This
typically happens every few weeks or months. We don't overtrade on daily noise.

### What returns can I expect?

Our goal is to **outperform a static buy-and-hold strategy** by avoiding large drawdowns (selling in
Greed) and buying more heavily at bottoms (buying in Fear). _Past performance is not indicative of
future results._

## üîí Security & Safety

### Is my money safe with Zap Pilot?

Yes, Zap Pilot is **non-custodial**.

- **Your keys, your coins:** Funds never leave your wallet until YOU sign a transaction.
- **No Lock-ups:** You can withdraw or exit the strategy at any time.
- **Transparency:** All logic is verifiable on-chain.

### What happens if Zap Pilot shuts down?

Your funds are in your wallet or in standard DeFi protocols (like Uniswap or Aave). You always have
direct access to them, even if our website goes offline.

### What are the fees?

We charge a simple **1% Entry Fee** when you first deposit into the strategy.

- **0% Management Fee**
- **0% Performance Fee**

### How do gas fees work?

You pay standard network gas fees for transactions. However, Zap Pilot optimizes these by spreading
execution over time and using efficient routing.
